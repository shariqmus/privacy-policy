We are committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use any of our apps.

Information Collection and Use

  • Our apps may collect limited technical data (such as device type, crash logs, and usage statistics) to help us improve performance and user experience.
  • Some apps may request access to device features or data (such as camera, storage, microphone, or location) only when necessary to provide the app’s core functionality.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or phone number) unless you explicitly provide it (for example, when contacting us for support).

Permissions

Different apps may request different permissions (such as camera or location). These permissions are used strictly for the intended functionality of the app and not for any unrelated purpose.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it as soon as possible.

Security

We value your trust and strive to use industry-standard measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
